The True Cost of a Shiba

My dog is pretty cute, albeit a bit shy. A lot of people like to ask: how much did she cost? What I generally infer they're asking is - what was the adoption fee?

The answer to that is $1600 CAD, albeit an incomplete one. I started breaking down all the costs associated with owning what many would consider to be a 'designer' breed from puphood. Here's what the real cost looks like:

Description Cost in $CAD
Adoption 1600
Food 300
Vet Expenses
Spaying 550
Vaccinations 240
Misc. Expenses 1600
Total
